Cordon Bleu

The most delicious Cordon Bleu you'll ever try

A cordon bleu is a dish that traditionally consists of meat wrapped around cheese, then breaded and pan-fried or deep-fried.

Many modern versions of the classic French recipe, such as this one, are baked.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken cutlets
  • Cooked ham
  • Swiss cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Simple spices

How to make a dish?

  1. Prep the chicken
  2. Assemble and Roll
  3. Bread and Bake

What to serve with Cordon Bleu

  • Mascarpone Mashed Potatoes
  • Easy Garden Green Beans
  • Gourmet Mushroom Risotto
  • Steamed Broccoli
  • Classic Restaurant Caesars Salad
