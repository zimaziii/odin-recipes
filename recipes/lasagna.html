Lasagna

The best classic lasagna recipe

The perfect lasagna recipe made with parmesan ricotta cheese feeling, melted mozzarella cheese, lasagna noodles and tomato meat sauce

It is the best lasagna recipe!

Ingredients:

  • meat
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • fresh parsley
  • dried basil leaves
  • black pepper
  • lasagna noodles
  • mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • ricotta
  • 1 egg

How to make a dish?

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. 4Layer the lasagna according recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
